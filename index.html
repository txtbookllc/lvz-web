After you download: Windows may show a blue warning

The first time you open the installer, Windows may show a full-screen blue “Windows protected your PC” message. This is normal and the app is safe. It happens because Low Vision Zoom is a brand-new app — Windows hasn’t seen it enough times yet to recognize it. The installer is digitally signed by txtbook LLC, our company.

To continue:

  1. Click More info on the blue screen.
  2. Check that the publisher line includes txtbook LLC.
  3. Click the Run anyway button that appears, and the installer opens as usual.

This warning fades on its own over the coming weeks as more people install the app. You only need to do this once.
